Discover the charm and beauty of my hometown.

Top 3 activities to do at León, Gto.

Eat a Guacamaya

The world-famous craving, consisting of a bolillo (a crusty Mexican bread roll), duro de puerco (fried pork skin), a spicy tomato sauce, lime, and salt.

Visit Bicentenario Theater

One of the country's most modern buildings, offering a wide variety of events.

Attend a match of team León

Go to the stadium that was part of the 1970 World Cup in Mexico and experience the thrill of watching the city's team play live.
